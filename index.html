Built for Real Financial Moments

fi-fore helps you tackle the jobs that matter most

When you're paid and need to know if you're on track...

See your actual spending vs your forecast in real-time. Auto-match transactions to your budget, spot variances instantly, and stay ahead without manual calculations.

When planning a big purchase or life change...

Run scenarios to see the impact before you commit. Test "what-if" situations with multiple budget scenarios, visualize cash flow 12+ months out, and make confident decisions.

When statements arrive and reconciliation feels like homework...

Match hundreds of transactions in seconds, not hours. AI-powered matching links forecasts to actuals automatically, while hierarchical categories keep everything organized.

When you need to understand where the money actually goes...

Drill into spending patterns across days, months, and years. Track budget rules (like 50/30/20), analyze credit spending separately, and identify trends before they become problems.

When organizing finances across multiple accounts...

See all your accounts—checking, credit, investments—in one view. Project balances forward, track statement reconciliations, and maintain a complete financial picture.

When importing bank data and dreading duplicate cleanup...

Import CSV files with built-in duplicate detection. Preview, edit, and validate transactions before import, with smart category suggestions from your history.

When privacy matters more than convenience...

Keep your financial data completely offline and local. No cloud sync, no data sharing, no subscription—just a desktop app that respects your privacy while delivering professional-grade financial insights.
